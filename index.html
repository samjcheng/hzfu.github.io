Brief Bio:

Hi! I am Huazhu Fu (付华柱). I am a Research Scientist in Institute for Infocomm Research (I2R), at Agency for Science, Technology and Research (A*STAR), Singapore. Earlier, I obtained my Ph.D. degree from Tianjin University (TJU), China, in 2013, and then worked as a research fellow at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, for two years.

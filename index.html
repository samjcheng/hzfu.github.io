Brief Bio:

I am the Senior Scientist with the Inception Institute of Artificial Intelligence (IIAI), Abu Dhabi, UAE. I received the Ph.D. degree from Tianjin University, China, in 2013, and then worked as the research fellow at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, for two years. From 2015 to 2018, I worked as a Research Scientist with the Institute for Infocomm Research, Agency for Science, Technology and Research (A*STAR), Singapore.
